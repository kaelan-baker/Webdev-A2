Our Moon Services

Our wonderful corporation VentureVector is the universe leader in lunar relaxation. You can’t prove otherwise! It’s a meaningless metric! We have three environments built up on the moon, the first building is the RecLab. This building hosts the sleeping pods and the rec-room. Our state of the art pods have been salvaged from the failed Voyager missions so they’re prime and ready for you to sleep like Our Nation's Heroes. The rec room has a ping pong table and multiple cabinets of Asteroids so you can even practice your aim for the Russian invasion.

The other two buildings include the Pool, and the Expedition Center. The Pool is a large indoor pool that is warmer than even the warmest hot springs here on Earth during the day. In the evenings the pool freezes and we have fresh ice to skate on every single night.

The Expedition Center holds our rover lab and the airlock. When you want to go for one of our amazing lunar nature walks or go do some exploration (or donuts) in our super built up Corvette Lunar Edition rovers.
